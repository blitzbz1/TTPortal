### Description (4000 chars max)
> TTPortal is the companion app for table tennis players. Discover venues, check in when you play, log your sessions, and meet players near you — all in one place.
>
> **Find your table.**
> Browse a map of table tennis clubs, public tables, and outdoor spots in your city. See opening hours, table count, lighting, and what other players say. Filter by city, rating, or what's open right now.
>
> **Track every session.**
> One tap logs that you played. Your play history, total hours, and city leaderboard rank update automatically. No spreadsheets, no clipboards — just tap and play.
>
> **Meet other players.**
> Add friends, see who's checking in at your favourite venues, and join open events near you. Organize matches with a few taps, set your own recurring sessions, or jump into someone else's.
>
> **Honest reviews from real players.**
> Rate venues on what actually matters — table condition, lighting, pricing, crowd. Photos and play history give context to every review.
>
> **Track your equipment.**
> Keep a log of the rubbers, blades, and balls you play with. Switch setups, note what worked, share your loadout with friends.
>
> **Climb the leaderboards.**
> Monthly challenges and city leaderboards reward consistency. Earn badges as you hit milestones.
>
> **Built for table tennis players, by table tennis players.**
> Available in English, Romanian, German, Italian, French, Spanish, Polish, and Czech.
>
> Free to use. No ads, no tracking — your play history is yours.

## Listing assets to prepare separately

These are not text; they're images/video that go alongside the copy above.

- **App icon** (1024×1024 PNG, no transparency, no rounded corners — both stores).
- **Feature graphic** (1024×500 PNG, Play Store only).
- **Phone screenshots** — at least 2, recommended 4–8 per store, per device size:
  - Play: 16:9 or 9:16, min 320 px short edge
  - App Store: 6.7" (iPhone 16 Pro Max) and 6.1" (iPhone 16) required
- **iPad screenshots** (12.9" or 13" iPad Pro) — required by App Store if `supportsTablet: true` (we have that).
- **App preview videos** (optional but boosts conversion ~15–25% on App Store).

Suggested screenshot order:
1. Map showing nearby venues with check-in pins
2. Venue detail with reviews + photos
3. Sessions / play history
4. Leaderboard / friends activity feed
5. Event detail / RSVP screen
6. Settings (highlight Privacy + Legal section as a trust signal)
